Hi, I'm Kelly. Thanks for the thanks.

I built Daily Builder Quotes because I kept reaching for builder wisdom and forgetting where I'd read it. Now it greets me every time I open a new tab — and I wanted to share it with you.

Why I made this

Lenny Rachitsky open-sourced 300+ podcast transcripts. Most of us never have time to listen end-to-end, but the best lines are the kind you want to re-read on bad days. I extracted one quote per day plus a plain-English unpacking — so two minutes a day gives you the punchline and the reasoning.

All quotes are real guest words from the transcripts, attributed and linked to their full episode. Nothing made up.
